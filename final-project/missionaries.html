Hoping to be called on a mission?

One of the main concerns temple missionaries have is being away from home, and being able to be allocated in a place that feels like home for a period of time and with all needs reachable. We offer:

  • Long-term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries
  • Exercise/workout room
  • Parking
  • Indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet access and smart TVs
